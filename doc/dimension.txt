

dimension_concept

   basic_dimension | named_dimension

dimension may be made of base_quantity_exps from different quantity_universe_types, 
 but not from different quantity_universes of same type
  for example dimension cannot be comprised of a base_dimension_exp from newtonian(physical type) and relativistic(physical type)
   but could be comprised of newtonian( physical type) and information(type information)
     

the quantity_system for each base_unit must be the same
